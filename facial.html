Constitutional Facial Rejuvenation Acupuncture

About:

In Traditional Chinese Medicine, the face reflects the overall balance and health of the entire body. Acupuncture is known as the medicine that treats the whole person – mind, body and spirit, and Constitutional Facial Acupuncture is not any different. It will not only addresses the signs of aging and stress that are reflected on the face, it also treats the entire body. Each treatment is individualized to the patient and will include body and facial points as well as Chinese herbal masks and organic moisturizing therapy. Fine needles are placed at a variety of acupuncture points on the face, neck and around the eyes to strengthen the muscles and stimulate the production and dispersal of collagen, which starts to break down as we age. The dual action of needles and the topical treatments stimulate blood circulation, tighten facial muscles, improve coloring and even out skin irregularities while improving a sense of well-being.

How many treatments?

Constitutional Facial Rejuvenation involves the patient in an organic process, in which a series of treatments is necessary to achieve maximal effect. Usually a series of 12-15 treatments is necessary but variables such as your age, lifestyle, current state of your skin, stress, diet, genetic inheritance, digestion, and sleep must be taken into account.

Treatment Timeline:

  • 1-2 treatments per week, 60 - 90 minutes

Maintenance Treatments:

Within the normal parameters of aging, the completion of a series of treatments should be effective. To ensure the persistence of the results, ongoing maintenance treatments are recommended:
  • Every 2 weeks for 2 months following the completion of a treatment series, then once a month for an indefinite amount of time
